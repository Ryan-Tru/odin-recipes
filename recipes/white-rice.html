White Rice:Stovetop Method

Description

Ingredients:

Stovetop:

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 1/2 cups water

Steps:

  1. Place water and rice in a large saucepan over medium high heat.
  2. Once the entire surface of the water is bubbling gently(and it gets a bit foamy, see video), turn heat down to low and cover with lid.
  3. Leave for 13 minutes - do not stir, do not remove lid. Water should now all be absorbed - tilt to check.
  4. Remove from stove, still with lid, and leave for 10 minutes.
  5. Remove lid, fluff with rice paddle or fork, and serve!
